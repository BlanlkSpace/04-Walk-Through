About Us

charity: water is a nonprofit organization bringing clean and safe drinking water to people in developing countries. Since 2006, charity: water has funded clean water projects for more than 17.5 million people around the world. Our mission is to end the water crisis in our lifetime by working with local partners to implement sustainable, community-owned water projects.
